IEEE WIE AG CEC

As Kerala Section established the first WIE Affinity Group in Region 10, College of Engineering Chengannur was more specific to bring down the initiative to its own lady members at the Student Branch level. The first IEEE WIE Affinity Group in Region 10 was initiated here on 7th February 2004, to identify the potential of WIE volunteers and to provide them with the best platform to gain experience. Through the years, IEEE WIE AG CEC has come a long way and has consolidated its position in the Kerala Section and Region 10 as one of the most vibrant Affinity Group.

Why ELLE?

ELLE'19 is up for bringing together, the women members of IEEE Women in Engineering under a roof to discuss and solve the common dilemmas of those delicate feminine minds. The event aims at lighting the minds and skills of enthusiastic girl students across Kerala and make them the torch bearers of the near future. This is sure to give them a major breakthrough into technical and managerial era, the industry demands.
